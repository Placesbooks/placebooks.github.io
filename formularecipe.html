Almond Cookies Recipe


Bake at 350 for 10 min. Check your cookies and bake at 345 for 3 min. Take out when they are golden.

Ingredients

1/3 Cup Almond Flour
1 Tsp Honey (add more if you want it sweeter)
1 Tsp Water (add more till the dough is wet)
Pinch Vanilla Extract Powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl. Add water as needed till you form a ball with your dough.


  2. Taste a tiny bit of your dough to make sure, you like the sweetness. You can add more almond flour if needed.


  3. Shape the dough into cookie balls and place them in a baking tray.


  4. Flatten the balls. You can create different shapes with them.


  5. Then, place your cookies in the oven and bake.


  6. Remove cookies from the oven, and wait till they cool.



Storage
I store mine in the freezer. When I’m ready to eat them again, I place them on the counter. Then, I wait a few minutes before I eat.
