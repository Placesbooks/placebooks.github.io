Vanilla Olive Shortbread Cookies

Take a bite of this sweet and tender,
Vanilla Olive Shortbread cookie.
Without butter, the olive oil adds a
clean, mellow nutty flavor that lingers
on your tongue. The vanilla wraps
through each bite like silk, and
the honey brings a touch of sweetness.

It’s not flashy. It’s graceful. A cookie that
whispers instead of shouts. It's quietly
indulgent, and the perfect treat to savor
slowly with tea in a sun-drenched room.

Ingredients: All Purpose Flour,
Olive Oil, Honey, Filtered Water,
Salt, Vanilla Extract, Baking Soda.
Allergen Information:
this food contains and is made in the facility with:
wheat, sesame, honey,
cocoa powder, cocoa butter, chocolate,
tree nuts, eggs, milk, shellfish, peanuts, soybeans.
Do not consume the food if you are allergic to these
ingredients.
