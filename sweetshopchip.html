Chocolate Chip Cookies

$ 20

Imagine holding this freshly baked chocolate
chip cookie. Bring it closer, and as you inhale,
let the rich, buttery, and sweet vanilla
chocolate aroma, wrap around your senses
like a cozy blanket.

Take a bite and let your teeth sink through
the tender top, and into the slightly chewy
center. The high quality chocolate is smooth,
and just a little bittersweet, perfectly
balanced against the sweet, slightly
caramelized dough. There’s a subtle crunch
from the edges, a softness in the middle,
and if you’re lucky a hint of sea salt
that makes the whole experience delightful.

It’s more than just a cookie.
It’s a moment of comfort, joy,
and nostalgia baked into every bite.

Ingredients:All Purpose Flour,
Butter, Cocoa Butter, Milk, Sugar,
Molasses, Salt, Water, Vanilla Extract,
Baking Soda, Unsweetened Chocolate,
Semi Sweet Chocolate Morsels
(Sugar, Chocolate, Milkfat, Cocoa Butter,
Soy Lecithin, Natural Flavor).
Allergen Information:
this food contains and is made in the facility with:
wheat, sesame, honey,
cocoa powder, cocoa butter, chocolate,
tree nuts, eggs, milk, shellfish, peanuts, soybeans.
Do not consume the food if you are allergic to these
ingredients.
