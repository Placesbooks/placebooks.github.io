The Importance Of Touch

I like to meditate because it keeps me present in the moment. I think touch, and hugs also help with becoming more aware. It’s important to have time reserved for you to feel at peace, and safe. I enjoy how it feels to be close to someone. The warmth and tenderness of their skin brings relief and comfort. I get the same feelings when I’m laying on the beach, watching the waves. Everything seems to move in harmony.
